## Bio (draft — conversational, authoritative, not corporate)
Joseph Ng'ang'a is the Founder and CEO of AfCEN (Africa Climate & Energy Nexus), where he's building AI-powered infrastructure that connects African energy projects to global capital. AfCEN's platform — spanning investment intelligence, regulatory monitoring, and satellite-verified project tracking — operates across 54 African markets.

Before AfCEN, Joseph built deep expertise at the intersection of energy project development, institutional finance, and digital systems across Africa and Europe. He currently advises governments, DFIs, and multilaterals on climate investment strategy, AI-powered organisation design, and African energy transition pathways.

Joseph is a sought-after speaker on AI for development finance, climate deal structuring, and the future of African capital markets. He has engaged with institutions including the African Development Bank, European Investment Bank, ECOWAS, UNDP, Enabel, Google X, and Africa50.

## Design Direction
- **Aesthetic:** Clean, minimal, modern. Dark mode preferred with subtle accent colours. Think: premium consulting/advisory feel — not startup, not NGO.
- **Typography:** Strong sans-serif headings (Inter, DM Sans, or similar), readable body text
- **Colour palette:** Dark navy/charcoal background, warm gold or amber accent, white text. Sophisticated, not flashy.
- **Inspiration:** Personal sites of people like Reid Hoffman, Dario Amodei, or high-end consulting partners
- **No stock photos.** Use geometric shapes, subtle gradients, or abstract data visualisations as design elements.
- **Mobile-first responsive design**

## Tech Stack
- **Next.js 14+** with App Router
- **Tailwind CSS** for styling
- **TypeScript**
- **Static export** (can deploy to Vercel, Netlify, or Cloudflare Pages)
- **No CMS needed yet** — content is hardcoded for now
- **Contact form:** Use Formspree or similar (no backend needed)
- Keep it simple — this should be deployable in minutes.

## Tone
The site should feel like walking into a quiet, well-appointed office. Confident without being loud. The work speaks for itself. Subtle animations on scroll are fine. No gimmicks.
