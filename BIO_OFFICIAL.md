# Joseph Ng'ang'a — Official Bio (from public domain sources)

## Current
- **CEO & Founder, Africa Climate and Energy Nexus (AfCEN)** — AI-powered platform unlocking investment into priority climate and energy solutions across Africa, operating across 54 markets
- **Chair, Funders Council at Sustainable Energy for All (SE4ALL)**
- **Governing Council Member, Sustainable Energy Fund for Africa (SEFA)**
- **Board Member, Carolina for Kibera**

## Former Roles
- **Mission 300 Special Envoy** — Official representative for AfDB and World Bank Group, engaging heads of state and ministers to secure Africa Energy Compacts for electrifying 300 million people by 2030. Appointed to support the initiative spearheaded by the World Bank Group, African Development Bank, GEAPP, SEforALL, and The Rockefeller Foundation.
- **CEO, inaugural Africa Climate Summit** — Appointed by H.E. President William Ruto of the Republic of Kenya
- **Vice President for Africa, Global Energy Alliance for People and Planet (GEAPP)**
- **Executive Director, Power & Climate Africa, The Rockefeller Foundation**
- **Co-founder & Executive Director, responsAbility Renewable Energy Holding (rAREH)** — Grew to a $121M company building, owning, and operating renewable energy projects across sub-Saharan Africa
- **Head, Regional Office for Africa, responsAbility A.G.** — $3B Swiss asset manager investing in Energy, Agriculture, and Financial Institutions
- **Founder, Renewable Energy Ventures (REV)** — Project development and advisory firm in Nairobi. Led development of the business plan and fundraising for the Kenya Climate Innovation Centre (KCIC), a $15M facility supporting clean energy entrepreneurs.
- **Advisor, AGRA's AECF (REACT window)** — Advising, supporting, and investing in clean energy companies
- **Investment Banker, Bank of America** — Charlotte, NC (career start)
