1. **Joseph's time is the constraint** — He's running AfCEN. Personal brand work needs to be high-leverage (speaking, retainers) not time-intensive (project work).
2. **AfCEN credibility is the moat** — Every engagement he does personally reinforces AfCEN's brand. They are synergistic, not competing.
